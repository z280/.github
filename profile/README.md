# z280

z280 is a collection of somewhat random sites and services managed by Zeke Neill and other contributors.

Most of this work is non-commercial and intended for the public benefit of citizens of Denver, Colorado, USA.

## What lives here

- Small public websites and utilities
- Community-oriented services and experiments
- Odds and ends maintained by contributors over time

## About this organization

The projects in this organization are varied by design. Some are long-lived, some are experimental, and some exist to support a specific community need. When there is a common thread, it is usually practicality, public usefulness, and a low-friction way to share work with others.

## Contributing

If you are here to help, look for the repository-specific guidance in each project. Contributions, fixes, and ideas that improve public benefit are welcome.
